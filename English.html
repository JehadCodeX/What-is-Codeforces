What is Codeforces?


        Codeforces is a popular online platform for competitive programming,
        offering contests and a wide variety of algorithmic problems.
        It hosts regular competitions for programmers of all skill levels,
        featuring problems on algorithms, data structures, and problem-solving 
        techniques. Users can track their performance through a rating
        system and interact with a global community
